When deciding which providers to see for your mental health services, it’s important to know how each different type of can help you.

Types of Mental Health Providers

Psychologists: Psychologist’s can evaluate your mental health and make diagnoses. They also run individual and group therapy sessions. These providers must have a doctorate.

Counselors/Therapists: These providers are masters level counselors that can assess a person’s mental health and use therapeutic techniques to lessen symptoms.

Psychiatrist: These providers are licensed doctors that can evaluate and make diagnoses, as well as prescribe and monitor medication.
