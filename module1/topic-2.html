Mental Health Resources before the ACA

Access to mental health services before the ACA was very limited and networked providers further limited access to care for mental health services. Out-of-Network providers are not contracted to accept the discounted rate that In-Network providers are contracted to accept. This means that patients run the risk of being billed a very high amount from the behavioral health provider with little coverage from the insurance.

This practice prevented patients from seeking the care that they needed because they were fearful of receiving an expensive bill.
